About

As a first-generation graduate and a passionate advocate for education, I am excited about the opportunity to further my academic and professional goals with the support of ALX scholarship. Growing up in a low-income household, I was determined to break the cycle of poverty and create a better life for myself and my family. Despite the challenges I faced, I remained committed to my education and worked hard to earn a spot at Federal University Dutsinma, Katsina State where I graduated with the cumulative grade point average of 4.53 in a 5-point grading system. As a Computer Science & IT major, I have been able to explore my interests and develop my skills in Computing, Analytics, Communication and Leadership, which I believe - will be valuable in my future career. In addition to my academic pursuits, I have enrolled in series of online classes, conferences and other environments that will enhance or support my goal. I have successfully completed some courses, attended some conferences while some; in view. I have so far acquired Statements of Accomplishment from DataCamp and Certifications from recognizable organizations like Google, Jobberman, Huawei, HP LIFE, to mention but a few - where I have gained valuable leadership and communication skills. Through these experiences, I have learned the importance of collaboration, empathy, and perseverance, skills that I believe will be essential in my future career. I have served as an Assistant Presiding Officer III in a general election held in Nigeria in the year 2019 under the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC). That same year, I registered not less than 600 candidates who sat for the Joint Admission and Matriculation Board (JAMB) which is a major requirement in most tertiary institutions in my home country. I am currently a volunteer in a sensitization programme in Nigerian indigenous languages for Citizens to understand their Civic Responsibilities and honoring the Nigerian National Symbols under the flag of "HIYA NIGERIA". Also, I am a Fellow with Teach for Nigeria trying all we can to eradicate education inequities.
